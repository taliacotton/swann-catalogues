A valuable reference to subsequent craftspeople, it has been reprinted, including in Diderot's <i>Encyclopedie</i>. Germain was a royal goldsmith, almost every design illustrated are his own, with the inclusion of several etchings of sketches by Jacques Roettiers (1707-1784). Roettiers was commissioned to create work for the Dauphin that was uncompleted at the time this book was published.
