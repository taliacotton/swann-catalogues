FOOTNOTE: "Handsome collection of scarce chromolithographs illustrating community life in the Pernambuco region of Brazil in the latter part of the nineteenth century. Francisco Henrique Carls (born Franz Heinrich Carls) emigrated from Germany to Brazil in 1858 and established his lithographic studio there in the 1860s. Plate counts found in Carls albums vary, sometimes with as few as 20.
