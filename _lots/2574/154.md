Recognized as one of the most innovative and prolific cartoonists and illustrators of his generation, Zann's work has appeared in magazines such as <i>Newsweek</i>, <i>Esquire</i>, <i>Fortune</i>; daily newspapers such as <i>The New York Times</i>; and in comics and on mystery covers.
