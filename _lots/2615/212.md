First offered in April 1885, a six-inch model sold for a dollar, while this twelve-inch version brought $5.00. The advertising circular (not included here) exhorted: \"Every American citizen should feel proud to donate to the Pedestal Fund and own a model in token of their subscription . . . in this great work.\"<br><br>At the bottom of the detached statue are the words \"American Committee Model,\" and at the top of the base are the faintly visible patent dates of 5 November 1878 and 18 February 1879.
