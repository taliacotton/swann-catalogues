TYPESET: "Ladislaus \"Ludwig\" Kmoch was a self-taught cartoonist and caricaturist who worked for the satirical magazines <I>Muskete</i> and <I>Simplicissimus</i>, until he joined the Social Democratic newspaper <I>Das Kleine Blatt</i> in 1929.  Kmoch designed this poster for the 1922 Viennese International Chess Congress, one of the first great tournaments after World War I, including all of the chess stars of the day. The black and white rooks stand monumentally like castles marking the entrance of Vienna, with a rendering of the Trade Fair Palace below the text."
