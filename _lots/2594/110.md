The first part of the album with images of military life and parades and gruesome scenes from the 1937 Battle of Shanghai, including images of the Honigsberg family outside the Cathay and Palace Hotels (the first American casualties of WWII), the bombing of the Wing On and Sincere department stores, and horrific war scenes of bomb victims and city ruins.
