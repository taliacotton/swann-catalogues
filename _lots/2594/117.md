Automatic cameras were mounted in towers on the lagoon and Crossroad pilots flew photo specific planes at a safe distance to capture the detonation stages of two atomic explosions, the Baker Day, and Able Day blasts. This selection of three panoramic images taken from Bikini Island depict both the Able and Baker atomic tests.
