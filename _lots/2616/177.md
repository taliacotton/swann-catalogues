Her expressive artworks explore identity and narrative, utilizing collage to articulate her experience as an African American female artist. She has taught art and art history in New Jersey at Essex County College for over 30 years and Bloomfield College for ten years.<br><br>Pickett‘s recent solo shows include The Matisse Series, Montclair Art Museum, Montclair, New Jersey, 2016.
