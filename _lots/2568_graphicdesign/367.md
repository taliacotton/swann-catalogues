Variety is achieved by the use of color, placement of the copy, and painted, drawn or ruled visual accents.\" Mildred Constantine, the Doyenne of Design from the museum's department of architecture and design, refers to this series as \"the most artistically significant institutional advertising design being done in America today.\"<i>Rare.</i> IPA 51 p. 340, MoMA 611.1951."
