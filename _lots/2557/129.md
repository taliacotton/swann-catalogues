Published by the New York WPA, with the \"New York City WPA Art Project\" stamp, lower left. Circa 1935.A very good impression.Besedick's artistic career showed early promise as he was awarded a scholarship from Scholastic Magazine to attend Vesper George Art School in Boston. Besedick also attended the National Academy of Design in New York and won a scholarship to study at the Tiffany Foundation on Long Island. From 1938 to 1939 he created prints for the WPA. During World War II, Besedick created illustrations for the Fourth Armored Division which were published. Besedick became a commercial artist after the war."
