Articles in this issue include \"The Negro Workingman at the Westinghouse,\" \"Development of Negro Business Ventures in Tidewater, Va.,\" sections on sports and theater, five short stories, and more. This issue bears the address label of Mrs. J.W. Burden of 1315 Adams Street--apparently Susie M. Burden, wife of John M., who was at that address in Grant, Indiana in the 1920 census.
