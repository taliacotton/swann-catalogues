  F. Burrall Hoffman, Jr., New York and Hobe Sound; private collection, Vermont.<br><br>According
  to Dr. James L. Yarnall, "This is one of La Farge's most experimental watercolors

  expressive and painterly surface effects that came to characterize his watercolors

  Waldo Emerson's poem, <em>The Sphinx</em> (this image was never published). Another
