TYPESET: An engaging Art Deco rendition of a bustling, fashionable day on Trouville's

  it "seems to encapsulate the essence of bright young thingery" (Hillier, p. 148).
  Executed in the manner of the fashionable Art Deco magazines of the era (Lauro was
  a magazine illustrator who specialized in fashion), this graceful, joyful and elegant
  celebration of the happy-go-lucky Roaring Twenties speaks to Lauro's mastery of
  his craft. Trouville / Deauville p. 63, Hillier p. 148 (var), Affiches Art Deco
