From 1925 to 1935, Andrews designed eight posters for the London Underground. Of these, six were sports-themed. She worked under the pseudonym Andrew Power as \"a tribute to Cyril Power, with whom she shared a studio and collaborated at the time\" (ltmcollection.org)."
