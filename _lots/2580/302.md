Sections include soups, salads, meat, fish, baked goods, desserts, and candies. The recipes are not exclusively Mexican--they also include French, Italian, and English cuisine--but many recipes feature distinctly Mexican ingredients and preparations, such as stuffed avocados (aguacates rellenos) on page 28, mole poblano on page 34, and quesadillas on page 79.
